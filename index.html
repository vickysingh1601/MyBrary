MyBrary

MyBrary - Iteration of Library is our application which we have made with utmost efforts This application has been created for students as well as teachers but most importantly for the ones who prefer books over e-books. Our application shows the count of the books still available in the library as it will reduce the work load of the students who go to the library just to see that their preferred book is not available. Using our application, students can search the name of their preferred book and see whether it is available for issue. It's a small effort to reduce to the work load of the students but a little goes a long way.
